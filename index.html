Maybe it is your case, maybe not, but it's certainly mine. You've spent years evaluating movies, albums, songs, books, ... and your favorite website disappears and with it all your ratings. Huge drama and enormous time wasted.

Let me give you another example. A fantastic and popular website, where you've spent years scoring films, does not implement a recommendation system (or at least a proper one), and you want to take your ratings elsewhere.

Seems legit.

My purpose is to implement an open source web application which will let you retrieve that information, do not forget it's yours.

The success and development of this idea will be evaluated not only economic, but also monitoring its support through social networks.

Also, depending on the type of content, we will consider improving the application so it may dump your ratings in any alternative site, studying if its treatment of your data is fair and ethical.

Stay tuned for updates.
